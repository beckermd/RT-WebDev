Barbara Comparini

Director

Paul leads the execution of the information technology solutions across Constellation’s client base.  He has over 30 years of experience in information technology implementation and support. From his numerous roles working with both large and small companies in the scientific, technical, consumer products, investment management, prime brokerage and other financial institutions.
