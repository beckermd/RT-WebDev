Barbara Comparini

Director

Paul leads the execution of the information technology solutions across Constellation’s client base.  He has over 30 years of experience in information technology implementation and support. From his numerous roles working with both large and small companies in the scientific, technical, consumer products, investment management, prime brokerage and other financial institutions. Paul brings a wealth of experience across a broad range of technological fields including application development, database development, network infrastructure, operations, project and portfolio management, system selection and implementation and user support. His extensive financial industry knowledge extends from vendor selection to the ongoing management of both onshore and offshore resources. Paul has extensive expertise advising Senior Management, CTO’s and CIO’s in the development of network and operational infrastructure and the implementation of disaster recovery plans.  Prior to joining Constellation in 2009, he spent nearly a decade providing portfolio management solutions to investment banks and fund managers. Paul holds a BA in Chemistry from Rutgers University, a Masters of Science in Management Information Systems from Lehigh University, and a Masters Certificate in Project Management from Villanova University.
